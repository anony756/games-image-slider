Ubisoft Montreal
Assassin's Creed
"Assassin's Creed" is a popular action-adventure video game series by Ubisoft, launched in 2007. It features players as Assassins fighting against Templars across various historical settings, from Renaissance Italy to Ancient Egypt. Known for its open-world exploration and stealth gameplay, the series explores themes of freedom versus control and has expanded into novels, comics, and a film, building a rich lore and a dedicated fanbase.

Call of Duty
"Call of Duty" is a highly popular first-person shooter video game franchise developed by various studios under Activision, first released in 2003. Initially set in World War II, the series has since expanded to include modern warfare, futuristic battles, and historical conflicts. Known for its fast-paced gameplay, multiplayer modes, and cinematic single-player campaigns, "Call of Duty" has become a cultural phenomenon, influencing the gaming industry and spawning numerous sequels, spin-offs, and esports tournaments.
Rockstar Games
Grand theft auto
"Grand Theft Auto" (GTA) is an acclaimed action-adventure video game series developed by Rockstar Games, first released in 1997. Set in open-world environments, the games allow players to explore fictional cities inspired by real-life locations, engaging in missions and side activities. Known for its immersive storytelling, satirical commentary on society, and freedom of gameplay, GTA has become one of the best-selling franchises in gaming history, with titles like "GTA V" achieving massive commercial success and cultural impact.

FarCry
"Far Cry" is a popular first-person shooter video game series developed by Ubisoft, first launched in 2004. The games are known for their vast open-world environments, often set in exotic locations, and a focus on exploration and survival. Players typically take on the role of a character facing off against various antagonists, utilizing a mix of stealth, combat, and strategy. The series is praised for its engaging storytelling, diverse gameplay mechanics, and stunning visuals, with each installment offering a unique setting and narrative, from tropical islands to the Himalayas.
